MY NAME IS KAMIL LELONEK

and I'm a full-stack developer, a polyglot programmer.

MY SERVICES

BACKEND

For me, working on the backend is the most exciting thing. I like to play with different languages and frameworks. At the moment, I'm focusing mainly on Elixir language and Phoenix framework.

DATABASES

My experience with DataBases includes working with PostgreSQL, Redis, Cassandra and EventStore. I'm able to configure both self-hosted and SaaS solutions. I used time series databases too.

INFRASTRUCTURE

I'm familiar with configuring custom infrastructure using Ansible or Chef. I always automate deployment and set up continuous integration. I follow infrastructure as code approach in my projects.

BLOCKCHAIN

Currently, I'm exploring Blockchain-based technologies. I'm building my own Blockchain implementation for educational purposes to teach others the entire concept.
